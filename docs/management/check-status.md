# Check Status

**Check Status** is used to check the status of the various services

Once selected, the various services and their statuses will be displayed. The important fields here is "running" and that the service is reported as being "healthy"

<figure><img src="/img/gitbook/Screenshot from 2025-06-02 16-08-57.png" alt="" /><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

Should the user wish to stop viewing the status, simply press `Ctrl+C` to stop.
